# ISO 11064‑4 Zero‑Cost Alternatives Research (2026‑01‑18)

## Question
What zero‑cost, publicly available alternatives can guide workstation/display layout decisions when ISO 11064‑4 is not available?

## Verified facts (official/public sources)
- The U.S. NRC publishes NUREG‑0700 Human‑System Interface Design Review Guidelines, with Revision 3 available for download and updated in 2025. This provides human factors guidance for HSI reviews, including control‑room design principles. (NRC)
- FAA publishes the Human Factors Design Standard (HF‑STD‑001) and provides a public download portal. This standard consolidates human factors criteria for FAA systems and facilities. (FAA HFDS)
- FAA also publishes “Human Factors Criteria for Displays” (DOT/FAA/TC‑07/11) as a free PDF that updates display design criteria. (FAA)
- NASA provides the Human Integration Design Handbook (HIDH) as a public PDF resource accompanying NASA‑STD‑3001, with human‑system design guidance. (NASA)

- These sources provide guidance, but do not replace ISO 11064‑4’s exact workstation dimension requirements; numeric constraints may still need ISO access or internal testing.
